About

Hi I’m Michael.

I’m a writer and typo enthusiast from New South Wales, Australia.

Despite bad hands, my words have appeared in The Guardian and the Newcastle Short Story Anthology. More recently, I studied creative writing at the University of New England.

I’m mostly interested in disability, accessibility and technology, and the way these overlap with fiction and pop culture. I also like animation, Cormac McCarthy and Earl Grey tea.

I’m currently co-writing a middle grade novel starring a disabled 12-and-a-bit-year-old heroine called Frances.
