23 May, 2024

An announcement and an apology.

Last month I got married. Surprisingly, given who I am as a person I was not anxious on the day, even when there was worry it would rain. I almost burst into hot tears the moment I saw Ceil in their dress and then again in the car, but I managed to keep it together for the ceremony. I was completely calm saying those words in front of people. It was a amazing day and even though it was overcast the gardens where we had ceremony were stunning. If you follow me on social media you will have already been bombarded with photos. And videos. Ciel has wonderfully documented the whole thing on Youtube. Needless to say but I’m saying it anyway, we are disgustingly happy and looking forward to many, many adventures together.

To anyone looking for a boyfriend, girlfriend, best friend and/or someone to marry, I am sorry. I got the best one. I don’t know how I did it. Or why I am so lucky. But I did it. And I plan on keeping them. So you’ll just have to make do.
