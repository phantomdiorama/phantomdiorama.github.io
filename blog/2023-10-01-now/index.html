Recently

01 Oct, 2023

The first half of the year (now 9 months) has been eaten up with more than usual ill health, which culminated in a night time ride in an ambulance and a overnight stay in hospital. Thankfully, things have started to level out enough that I can concentrate on other things.

We managed to get Frances out to some folks a few months ago and honestly I’m find the whole beta reading thing kinda frustrating. Not because of the lovely people who decided to take it on. They are total sweethearts. But because it turns out I’m a very impatient person; I both want and dread feedback. Being extra sick and having else to focus on hasn’t helped.

And a recent visit from my datefriend has cheered me up immensely. We’re long distance and they’re a teacher so we really only get to see each other during the school holidays. It’s good to just be around their energy and we got to start planning for the upcoming wedding. We’re looking at venues and celebrants. I’ve already bought my wedding clothes including the brightest socks in the whole world. It’s very exciting.

You may have noticed the site has had a redesign. I’m doing it mostly by hand now. You will need to re-subscribe to the feed. Sorry about that.

reading
