Bonus: Un-Pocket

Also last time, I wanted to convert the list of links exported from Pocket into full text copies. That turned out to be pretty easy–I just needed htmlq. Then to get an unformatted list of links, I ran this in git-bash on Windows:

cat export.html | htmlq --attribute href a | sed '/^http/!d' > pocket.txt

And ran the list through Jones:

while IFS='' read -r line; do
    jones "$line"
done < pocket.txt
