about me

I am a houseplant. Poor health necessitates being a homebody, so I spend most of my time at home with a ham-fuelled cat, either reading, writing or playing Animal Crossing with my datefriend.

Together we are currently writing a middle grade novel starring a disabled 12-and-a-bit-year-old heroine called Frances. She’s got a big heart and a walking stick.

When I’m not writing, I’m drinking tea. I’ve been a tea drinker for years and have tried every style and type available at the supermarket. But it wasn’t until the plague that I really got into peppermint tea. And now it’s my favourite. I’m still only at teabag level but see myself venturing into the wild west of loose leaf.
