what I’m doing now

Updated September 15, 2024, the beginning of the Australian spring.

After letting her sit in a drawer for almost a year we’re getting ready to embark on a deep edit of Frances, the book we’re writing, which feels very much like attempting to drag a ship over a mountain–we have the ropes and the logs and no idea where to start.

However, that’s already had to take a beakseat, as we have made the decision to have a baby. And to use science to do so. Which, you can imagine, is many ships over many mountains.
