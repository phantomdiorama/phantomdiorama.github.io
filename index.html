Michael Buckley

Writer. Activist. Riverboat Gambler.

Michael Buckley, 2012Michael is an Australian writer who, despite bad hands, writes about disability and politics. His words have appeared in The Guardian and others. He also writes short fiction about sad people. He is currently working on his first novel.
